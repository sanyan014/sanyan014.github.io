I used to really enjoy spicy food when I was a kid. However, due to an eventful trip in high school, I am no longer able to eat spicy food. This is such a shame because I loved eating spicy food so much. Especially since most Asian cultures, like Thai, Chinese, and Burmese, revolve around extremely spicy dishes and flavorful cuisines. To this day, I am still training my palate to return back to normal so I can indulge myself in spicy meals again. In Boston, there are some places that remind me of the spicy dishes from back home. Thai and Chinese restaurants like 5 Spices, Rod Thai, Thai Basil, and Rod D are just a few of the superb restaurants for Asian cuisine that offer dishes filled with rich flavors that are quite literally eye-opening. Though I can only eat the mildest spice range of every dish, I am still somewhat able to enjoy the delicious dishes from the restaurants, which provide an array of flavors that tantalize my taste buds. I often find myself reminiscing about the bold flavors and heat I used to relish, hoping to one day return to enjoying spicy food fully again.

My Favorite Spicy Dishes

  • Spicy Thai Pad Kra Pao
  • Hot Pot
  • Mapo Tofu
  • Spicy Kimchi Fish Cakes
